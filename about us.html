historical Background

The Muassasat Nasrul Ilm Wa Da’awat Foundation (MNIDF) traces its origins to the African American Islamic Institude (AAII),founded by Imam Hassan Cisse (RTA) in 1988 in the United States of America. The mission, widely recognized as Muassasat Nasrul Ilm in africa, become a pivotal force in spreading islam in west. Through Imam Hassan's efforts, many Americans embraced islam, and under his guidance, Qur'an was recorded- a remarkable achievement (Allahu Akbar!).

MNIDF’s presence in Nigeria dates back to the lates 1980s, particularly in the South-West geopolitical Zone and oter parts of the country.The organization steadily grew, establishing structured executive leadership at National, State, and Branches levels,ensuring widespread reach and impact.

A milestone in the foundation's journey was the 2004 Conference at the Federal Palace Hotel,where Imam Hassan Cisse (RTA) formally introduced the name " Nasrul Ilm ", affirming its legacy. In his speech, he referenced a letter from his grandfather, Sheikh Ibrahim Niass (RTA) , Who had originally founded the mission in 1940 and entrusted its leadership to Imam Hassan. The Sheikh's vision called on Muslims From Africa to India and beyondto contribute to the mission, urging males to danate two dirhamsand females one dirham as a means of financial sustainability and collective success.

Addressing the economics challenges faced by Muassasah and its members in Nigeria,Imam Hassan (RTA) provided a profound message of self-reliance and collective effort. He emphasized that rather than lamenting financial constraints, Muslims should unite,contribute what they can, and build a strong foundation through collective action.He reminded his followers that Allah grants success to those who strive and that their work was for His sake alone.His words remain an enduring inspiration:

" Be for Allah and be together, and Allah will be for you. "

MNIDF’s was formally incorporated in Nigeria in April 2019 under the leadership of Sheikh Usman Adebayo Giwa (RTA) to further its mission of knowledge dissemination, humanitarian service, and community development.

Our Foundation

Promoting Knowledge and Empowerment for a Better World

The Muassasat Nasrul Ilm Wa Da’awat Foundation (MNIDF) is a global Islamic non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to the propagation of Islamic knowledge, humanitarian aid, and community development. Inspired by the Tijaniyyah Sufi Order, our foundation is committed to the Uplifting communities through education, empowerment, and spiritual enlightenment. We believe in fostering a society where knowledge nd service go hand in hand to alleviate poverty and enhance the well-being of the muslim Ummah.

Core Values

1
Compassion: Supporting the vulnerable with love and care.
2
Knowledge: Promoting education as a tool for empowerment.
3
Sustainability: Building long-term, impactful solutions.

Our mission

To promote balanced Islamic education, alleviate poverty, and create a healthy, empowered, and united society through impactful initiatives.

Our vision

To build a knowledgeable, self-reliant, and spiritually enriched global community driven by compassion and collaboration.

Our Journey So Far

Established in 2005, MNIDF began as a small community-driven initiative focusing on education. Over the years, we’ve expanded our reach across Nigeria, impacting thousands of lives through scholarships, humanitarian aid, and empowerment programs.

2005

Foundation established with a focus on education.

2010

Launch of the first scholarship program for underprivileged students.

2015

Initiated large-scale humanitarian relief efforts during national crises.

2020

Introduced the Youth Empowerment Initiative.
