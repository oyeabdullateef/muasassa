About MNIDF

Muassasat Nasrul Ilm Wa Da’awat Foundation (MNIDF)

is a global Islamic NGO inspired by the Tijaniyyah Sufi Order, committed to promoting balanced Islamic education, alleviating poverty, and fostering spiritual growth. Guided by our core values of service, knowledge, unity, and spiritual growth, we deliver impactful programs including education, empowerment, and humanitarian aid. Our current and future projects include scholarships, zero-interest financial platforms, relief packages, and the development of an Islamic center with facilities like a mosque, school, and hospital.

Learn More

Core Values

Guided by principles of service, knowledge, unity, and spiritual growth, MNIDF fosters growth and empowerment for all.

Knowledge-Driven

Serving humanity through charity, empowerment programs, and community development initiatives.

Humanitarian Service

Promoting authentic Islamic teachings based on the Qur'an, Sunnah, and Tijaniyyah principles.

Unity

Strengthening bonds within the Muslim Ummah to promote collaboration, peace, and collective progress.

Spiritual Growth

Inspiring faith development through Tijaniyyah Sufi practices, fostering deeper connections with Allah.
